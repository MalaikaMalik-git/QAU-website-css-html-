MPhil (Computer Science)

The objective of M.Phil Computer Science programme is to train students to initiate research work. The duration of M.Phil programme is two years (4 semesters).

MPhil is of 50 credit hours, 24 credit hours for the course work and 26 credit hours for research work. Each course is of 3 credit hours. There are 2 compulsory courses, and 6 optional courses are taken to complete 24 credits. The courses are designed in such a way that during the course work students will be exposed to research activities. The course work will set the basic ground work to start research.

MPhil University Policy/Rules Fee structure Details about Admissions

Students will register for a research topic in the 3rd semester and will complete the research work by the end of the 4th semester. The research work will be submitted in the form of a thesis.
